The Store

Elsalg Pandrup has been the premiere electrical appliance store since 1994. We provide help with, reparations, installation and the purchase of new appliances.

Elsalg primary handles larger appliances but also has an assortment of lamps, alarms and other accessories for the everyday handyman

All purchases will be handled physically, whether you pick up the appliance at the store or whether we deliver and install it is up to you!

Elsalg

The chain is owned by A / S El-Salg, which is a public limited company founded in 1968, today more than 160 stores throughout the country - often in the local areas. In 2018, the El-Salg chain also became part of the Euronics chain's international purchasing collaboration, which means that the local El-Salg stores are already shopping together with the other 11,000 stores in the Euronics chain.
