Reparation services

Washing Machine

A well mentioned washing machine can last between 7 and 13 years. Main reasons they break are overheated motor, foreign objects, overloading or dirty dispenser.

Coffee Machine

With proper maintenance a coffee machine should last you around 5 years, the main reason it brakes usually being lack of proper maintenance.

Fridge

The average lifespan of freezers and refrigerators is between 14 and 17 years. The main part that can brake in a fridge is the compressor.

Oven

On average an electric oven should last you 13 years. The main cause for a broken oven is overheated resistance elements due to a voltage overload.

Microwave

On average, a microwave could last you around 8 years. The most common issue that may appear is the plate not spinning.

Fans

A fan is a piece of household that can last a very long time. The main reason the might brake is usually a short-circuit.
